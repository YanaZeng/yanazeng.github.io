Biography

I am a Lecturer at Beijing Technology and Business University (BTBU) in China. I am fortunate to work with [Shohei Shimizu](https://sites.google.com/view/sshimizu06/) as a visiting scientist of RIKEN, Japan. Before joining BTBU, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University (2021 - 2023), supervised by Prof. [Fuchun Sun](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/info/1312/4393.htm), working on causal reinforcement learning. I obtained my ph.D. degree at Guangdong University of Technology (2016 - 2021), supervised by Prof. [Zhifeng Hao](https://scholar.google.com.sg/citations?user=ZF3gp9wAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN), and Prof. [Ruichu Cai](https://ruichucai.github.io/), and co-supervised by Prof. Shohei Shimizu (RIKEN&Shiga University). From 2019-2020, I was a visiting Ph.D. student in the causal inference group of RIKEN.
